OnNetworkReady(ulong)

This method is subscribed to Unity's NetworkManager.OnClientConnectedCallback. So, it runs when NetworkManager has started up (following a succesful connect on the client, or directly after StartHost is invoked on the host). It is named to match NetworkBehaviour.OnNetworkSpawn, and serves the same role, even though GameNetPortal itself isn't a NetworkBehaviour.
